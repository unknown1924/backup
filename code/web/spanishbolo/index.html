Our Methodology

Study Spanish at any level, from beginner to expert.

Following CEFR guideline for European languages, we offer you a complete Spanish course from beginner to mastery level.

How do we teach?

Everyone has different language learning aptitudes, believe us! At the outset we try to analyse the way you will learn maximum and at the fastest pace. Our target is not to deliver a certain hour of rote classes, but to focus on the learning output. Remember, the entire Spanish language grammar or any grammar can be taught just in a month. But, can a human brain handle it? Never! Give the brain sufficient amount of time to process the input.

We blend the traditional teaching methodologies; grammar translation method, contrastive grammar, direct method, communicative method etc with the ICT tools for language learning and applications, such as Duolingo, Memrise, Quizlet with free Spanish Learning materials available online. Moreover, an easy learning Spanish grammar textbook is also used to solidify the learning. Hence, you get a guaranteed result from our teaching input.
